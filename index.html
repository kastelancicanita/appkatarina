AktivnostiActivities
  • 7 km plaže i šetnice od Mutograsa do Strožanca — idealno za šetnje, trčanje i biciklizam uz more.
  • Brdo Perun u neposrednoj blizini — planinarske staze i panoramske šetnje iznad Podstrane.
  • Crkva sv. Jure (stara preko 1000 godina) — povijesna sakralna baština iznad Gornje Podstrane.
  • Gornja Podstrana — predivan pogled na more, otoke i Split.
  • Vodeni sportovi — preporučujemo Argola Experience (jet ski, sea safari).
  • Povezanost s otocima iz Splita: Brač (~20 km), Šolta (~25 km), Hvar (~40 km), Vis (~55 km) — redovne trajektne i katamaranske linije iz trajektne luke Split (vožnja do luke cca 20 min).
  • 7 km of beaches and seaside promenade from Mutogras to Strožanac — perfect for walks, jogging and cycling by the sea.
  • Perun Hill nearby — hiking trails and scenic walks above Podstrana.
  • Crkva sv. Jure (over 1,000 years old) — historic heritage site above Gornja Podstrana.
  • Gornja Podstrana — beautiful views of the sea, islands and Split.
  • Water sports — we recommend Argola Experience (jet ski, sea safari).
  • Island connectivity via Split port: Brač (~20 km), Šolta (~25 km), Hvar (~40 km), Vis (~55 km) — regular ferry and catamaran lines from Split Ferry Port (approx. 20 min drive to the port).
